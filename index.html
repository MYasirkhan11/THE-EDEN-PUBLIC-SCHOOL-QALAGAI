About Us

The Eden Public School Qalagai offers education from Nursery to 10th grade. We are committed to providing quality education with the best teaching staff in a co-education environment. Our school is equipped with modern facilities including a transport system and a canteen.

Message from the Principal

Welcome to The Eden Public School Qalagai. Our mission is to foster an environment of excellence in education, where every student is encouraged to reach their full potential. We take pride in our dedicated teaching staff and comprehensive curriculum.
